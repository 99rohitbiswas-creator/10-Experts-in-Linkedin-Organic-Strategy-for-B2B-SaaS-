### 1. The ACE Framework Distribution Map
**Source:** [LinkedIn - ACE Intro](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexander-j-lewis_most-of-the-highly-technical-b2b-companies-activity-7315719742948839424-9NxG)
**Date:** Oct 24, 2024

B2B SaaS buys are "trust buys." You must distribute the CEO's ideas across three specific layers:

* **A (Authority):** Long-form, high-depth "anchors" (Monthly Memos, Whitepapers). 
    * *100Hires Goal:* Prove the CEO is a deep thinker on hiring logistics.
* **C (Conversational):** LinkedIn posts serving as a feedback loop. 
    * *100Hires Goal:* Test small components of your "Authority" content to see what resonates.
* **E (Earned):** Borrowed credibility (Podcasts, Keynotes). 
    * *100Hires Goal:* External platforms invite you because your Authority content is top-tier.

## 2. Moving Beyond "Ticking the Box"
**Source:** [LinkedIn - MD "Ticking the box"](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexander-j-lewis_i-spoke-with-a-managing-director-last-week-activity-7429591783212269569-Vzx9)
**Date:** Jan 14, 2025

Stop treating social media as a chore and start treating it as a **knowledge system**.

* **The Logic:** Social media is the "Conversational Layer." 
* **The Shift:** Stop posting "3 Tips for Hiring." Start posting **Field Notes**—observations from actual sales calls, product meetings, and candidate frustrations.

## 3. Momentum as Social Proof
**Source:** [LinkedIn - Momentum Marketing](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexander-j-lewis_in-executive-led-marketing-momentum-social-activity-7358224410429853696-Q3g1)
**Date:** Aug 14, 2024

In SaaS, buyers don't just buy features; they buy **momentum**.

* **The Core:** People follow motion. If you look like you are winning, trust increases.
* **Execution:** Use "understated confidence." Don't shout about a release; narrate the *impact* of the motion.
* *100Hires Example:* "We noticed candidates were dropping off at stage 2. We tweaked the workflow yesterday; today, conversion is up 20%. Motion matters."
