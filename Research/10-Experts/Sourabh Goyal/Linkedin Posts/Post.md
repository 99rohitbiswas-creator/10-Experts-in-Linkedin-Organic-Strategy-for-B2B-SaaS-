## 1. The "Problem-First" Content Shift
Most SaaS founders optimize for "Expert Status" or "Virality," which results in zero leads. Sourabh advocates for a fundamental shift in perspective:

* **The Mirror Rule:** Your audience doesn't care how much you know. They care if you understand their current reality.
* **The Solution Assumption:** If you can articulate a pain point with extreme precision, the prospect automatically assumes you have the solution.

## 2. The Content-Profile Pipeline
Content gets the attention, but the **Profile** converts it. A high-performing profile must act as a landing page that answers three questions instantly:
1. **Who** do you help?
2. **What** specific problem do you solve?
3. **How** do I work with you?

## 3. The Audit Findings (SaaS Profile Fixes)
After auditing 10 SaaS profiles, Sourabh identified that "Posting More" is rarely the answer. Instead, founders must:
* **Sharpen Positioning:** Stop being a "generalist" and become "painfully specific."
* **Optimize for Inbound:** Treat the headline and featured section as a conversion funnel, not a resume.
* **Quality > Quantity:** 10 posts with the right positioning outperform 100 posts of "corporate fluff."
