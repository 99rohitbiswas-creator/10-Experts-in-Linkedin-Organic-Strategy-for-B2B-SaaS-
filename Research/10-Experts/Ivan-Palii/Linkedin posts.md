## Top-performing / high-signal LinkedIn posts (early 2026)

### 1) 3 trends that broke organic growth on LinkedIn (and how I adapt)

- **Timing shown on LinkedIn**: “1mo” (captured 2026-04-27)
- **Link**: `https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ivanpalii_3-trends-that-broke-organic-growth-on-linkedin-activity-7438596055044206592-Gf4S`
- **Key excerpt (verbatim)**:
  - “3 trends that broke organic growth on LinkedIn (and how I adapt to a new reality) … You have to consider organic activity on them only as part of your overall marketing strategy.”
  - “1/ More and more people are posting… this increases the overall competition for attention.”
  - “2/ Platforms have shifted to promoting content based on interests and engagement… each of your posts is either a hit or a miss.”
  - “3/ Using AI for commenting and posts… In my opinion, AI simplifies gaining visibility, but kills reputation.”
  - “Posts from influencers with 30k-50k followers often get the same number of views as those with 3k-5k followers… if a post gets 1000 views now, that’s a good result now.”
- **Why it matters for B2B SaaS**:
  - Treats LinkedIn as **one layer** of the growth system, not the system.
  - Warns against “AI comments” as a brand risk—visibility vs. reputation tradeoff.

### 4) “GSC rank tracking” idea: track 1,000 keywords cheaply via Search Console

- **Timing shown on LinkedIn**: “2mo” (captured 2026-04-27)
- **Link**: `https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ivanpalii_have-you-ever-dreamed-of-a-rank-tracker-where-activity-7431708869380337664-1RAH`
- **Key excerpt (verbatim)**:
  - “Have you ever dreamed of a Rank Tracker where you can track thousands of keywords 10 times cheaper? … based on using Average Position data by search queries from Search Console.”
  - Proposed workflow: add keywords + target URLs, store positions as GSC updates, then report trends, wrong-URL rankings, impression-less keywords, group trends.
  - “Popular tools… charge near $50-$100 per 1k tracked keywords. Imagine that you can track 1k keywords for $5 or $10.”
  - “Rank Tracker: for the 100 most valuable keywords… GSC Rank Tracker: for 1000 most valuable keywords and looking for content gaps.”
- **Why it matters for B2B SaaS**:
  - Practical way to scale “visibility ops” when SERP scraping becomes harder/more expensive.
  - Bridges SEO reporting + prioritization into a productized analytics workflow.
