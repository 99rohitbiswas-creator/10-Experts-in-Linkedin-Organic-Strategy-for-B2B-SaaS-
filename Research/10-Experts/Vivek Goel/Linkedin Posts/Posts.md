## The 10 Principles of SaaS Success

1. **Specific Problem Solving:** Solve high-friction pain points.
2. **Resilient Foundations:** Bootstrap early to ensure capital efficiency.
3. **Global Market Entry:** Don't wait to scale internationally.
4. **Vertical Moats:** Specialized solutions beat general tools in high-stakes industries.
5. **Ecosystem Evolution:** Shift from product to platform for long-term viability.
6. **Retention Mastery:** Customer success is the engine of SaaS revenue.
7. **Organic Growth:** Use content and community to lower CAC.
8. **AI Integration:** Use AI as a core strategic differentiator.
9. **UX Excellence:** Design is a competitive advantage.
10. **Agile Pivoting:** Recognize pivots as natural growth stages.
