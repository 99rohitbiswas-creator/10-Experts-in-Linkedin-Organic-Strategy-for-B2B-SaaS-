### 4) “Clarity before distribution” (AI visibility is a clarity problem)

- **Post timing shown**: 3w (as of 2026-04-27)
- **Link**: `https://www.linkedin.com/posts/concurate_everyone-wants-to-show-up-on-ai-surfaces-activity-7445043261720858624-ECPz`
- **Why included**: It cites Anjali’s thinking and frames Concurate’s operating principle; it’s directly aligned with “thought leadership + sales alignment.”
- **Hook**: “If your own website can’t clearly explain what you do… don’t expect AI systems (or journalists, or buyers) to figure it out.”
- **Core logic**:
  - Visibility isn’t only distribution/PR; it starts with **positioning clarity**:
    - what you do, who it’s for, why different, how it works.
  - AI visibility fails when the company story is “slick but empty.”
- **Framework** (Clarity Checklist, pre-PR/pre-LinkedIn):
  - **What do you do?**
  - **Who is it for?**
  - **What problem do you solve?**
  - **How does it work?**
  - **Why should anyone care?**
  - If these aren’t obvious, fix messaging before “AI strategy.”
