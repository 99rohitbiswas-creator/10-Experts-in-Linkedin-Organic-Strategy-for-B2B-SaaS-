## 🎯 The "Universal Success" Framework
Despite geographic and industry differences, these 10 experts align on five non-negotiable principles for B2B SaaS growth:

### 1. Personal Profiles > Company Pages
The data is unanimous: Personal profiles (Founders/CEOs) earn significantly higher reach, engagement, and trust. People want to buy from people, not faceless entities.

### 2. Niche Depth > Broad Reach
Generic "business advice" is discarded. Success is found in solving high-friction, technical problems for a hyper-specific **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**. The goal is to be "meaningfully specific" rather than "broadly relevant."

### 3. The "Long Game" Mentality
Organic growth is treated as a compounding asset, not a short-term hack. The typical timeline for a "Lead Generation Engine" to stabilize and drive consistent inbound inquiries is **6 to 12 months**.

### 4. Strategic Engagement
Publishing content is only 50% of the work. Visibility is driven by **"Comment Hijacking"**—leaving thoughtful, high-value responses on the posts of industry leaders and potential customers.

### 5. Authentic Human Narrative
Polished brand marketing is less effective than human storytelling. Experts prioritize:
* **Building in Public:** Sharing raw lessons from failures.
* **Behind-the-Scenes:** Documenting high-stakes decision-making.
* **The "Founder's Voice":** Using a tone that is relatable yet authoritative.
